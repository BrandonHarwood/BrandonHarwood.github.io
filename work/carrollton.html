City of Carrollton
I was brought onto the marketing team for The City of Carrollton in order to help the lead graphic designer work on multiple projects at once. Here are a few of them.

Carrollton Trails App:

The city recently released their first mobile app so citizens can find and navigate through the city's multiple trails, I was asked to create a poster to advertise the new app!



Go Vote Carrollton:

Everyone knows the youngins' these days have a bit of trouble knowing when it's time to vote, so I creatd this advertisement to raise awareness.



Tree of Life:

This illustration was based off of a painting currently residing in the Carrollton animal services center, I created the vector so it can forever live in the vast ocean of the internet.
