Hey there, my name's Brandon.
And this is my girlfriend pretending to not be annoyed with me.

I'm a senior studying psychology at the University of North Texas pursuing a career in HCI and user experience design/research. I absolutely love learning about the ergonomics of the web and applying research to design.

I’m currently working with UNT’s IT Shared Services team designing their service website’s user interface. Alongside that I’m also the Founder & Presidents of UNT’s Association for Design & Code!

See my resume for more info!

A bit more about me:
I’m the kinda guy that looks at something that’s just “ok” and doesn’t think it’s good enough. I like to back up my decisions with data, and I enjoy the finer things in life. I’m a fan of good coffee, beer, and wine, and of course, I try to sprinkle in a little adventure here and there. When I'm not scrambling around putting together website while or running an organization or taking care of my roommate's dogs, I like to play scary video games, shoot some photos, travel.

A bit more about D&C:
The Association for Design & Code is dedicated to bringing engineers and designers together early on in their career to work on projects together and learn a bit more about the other side of development. We also host speakers and workshops to help people learn the fundamentals of app and website development! In Fall 2016, we plan on throwing Hack North Texas; a nation wide hackathon promoting the collaboration between designers and engineers.
